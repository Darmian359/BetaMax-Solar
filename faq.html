Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I save with solar panels?

Most customers save 50–80% on their electricity bills depending on system size, location, and energy consumption. We provide detailed ROI calculations during consultation.

How long do solar panels last?

Quality solar panels last 25–30 years with minimal degradation. Most panels retain about 80% efficiency after 25 years.

What happens during cloudy days?

Solar panels still generate electricity during cloudy weather, though at reduced efficiency. Battery systems help store power for consistent usage.

Can solar power run my entire home?

Yes, depending on system size and battery capacity, solar can fully power your home. We design systems tailored to your energy needs.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations take 1–3 days depending on system size.
